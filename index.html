Reslife Transition

Hi. Welcome to the binder.

A residential life handoff site for Jester at UT Austin — built by Rain.

This is a living resource for the student leaders who keep Jester running. If you're an ACC, RA, Senior RA, or on RHC — there's a tab for you. Open one of the windows on the desktop to start, or use a shortcut below.

  • Transition Reports ACC, RA, Senior RA, RHC
  • Resources Boards, decs, events

How to use this site: click an icon on the desktop to open a window. Each window has tabs at the top — click to switch sections. Drag windows by their title bars. Bookmark the desktop and you'll come back to where you left off.

— A note from Rain: I built this so you wouldn't have to start from a blank page like I did. Steal anything. Improve everything. Add your own tab when something here goes stale.
